To convert HEX to BIN as A NATURAL UNSIGNED DECIMAL NUMBER:
take the HEX NUMBER SYMBOL
0xF123
Separate and convert each symbol to bin:
F - 1111
1 - 0001
2 - 0010
3 - 0011
ANSWER: HEX(F123) = BIN(1111 0001 0010 0011)

To convert BIN to HEX
Devide BINARY number by 4 symbols from right side
101001 lets take as an example number
which will not devide perfectly by 4
in this case just divide and process rest
part as is:
Deviding 101001 into 10 1001:
10 - 2 in decimal - 2 in hex
1001 - 9 in decimal - 9 in hex
result: BIN(101001) = HEX(29)

another example
A1 in HEX
A = 10 in decimal = 1011 in bin
1 = 1 in decimal = 1 in bin
as in HEX numbers should be taken
from right side to the left with in
interval of 4 symbols, we should
consider this information when preparing
